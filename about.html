About Us

At Bali Fashions, we are more than just a textile manufacturer—we are a legacy of craftsmanship, consistency, and customer trust built over three decades.

Founded and proudly managed by the family of Mr. Prakash Haria, Bali Fashions has grown into a respected name in the textile industry, serving both local and regional markets with distinction. From our base in Baba Dogo Light Industrial Area, Nairobi, we specialize in the manufacture of high-quality uniforms, advertising materials, and safety products tailored to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our strength lies in our unwavering commitment to quality, precision, and design excellence. With a team of over 50 skilled and experienced professionals—many of whom have been with us for years—we ensure every product reflects superior craftsmanship and attention to detail.

At Bali Fashions, customer satisfaction is the foundation of our business. Our clients’ continued trust, positive feedback, and strong recommendations are a testament to our dedication to service excellence.

As we look ahead, we are focused on growth and expansion, with plans to establish additional branches both locally and regionally to better serve our growing clientele.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality textile products through innovative design, skilled craftsmanship, and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be a leading textile manufacturer in Africa, recognized for quality, reliability, and excellence in service delivery.
